Deadlock prevention is the mechanism to ensure that at least one of the necessary conditions for
deadlock can never occur. On the other hand, deadlock avoidance is the mechanism to ensure that the
system does not enter an unsafe state. Thus, this is the main difference between deadlock prevention
and deadlock avoidance. (https://pediaa.com/what-is-the-difference-between-deadlock-prevention-and-deadlock-avoidance)
